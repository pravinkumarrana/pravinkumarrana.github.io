Projects

Industry Projects – Eye Tracking & Extended Reality (XR)

  • Tobii XR Eye Tracking Platform (Meta Butterscotch Varifocal) – Advanced eye-tracking platform enabling varifocal rendering for next-generation XR headsets.
  • Sony PlayStation VR2 – Integrated eye-tracking technology into a high-volume consumer gaming headset, enhancing immersion and user experience.
  • HP Reverb G2 Omnicept Edition VR – Delivered biometric-driven eye-tracking features for enterprise-grade VR solutions.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon XR2 5G Platform – Contributed to XR reference designs, optimizing real-time eye-tracking performance on mobile chipsets.
  • Pico Neo 3 Pro Eye – Supported development of integrated eye-tracking for standalone VR systems.
  • Pico Neo 2 Eye – Developed eye-tracking modules that led to recognition as one of TIME’s Best Inventions 2020.
  • HTC Vive Pro Eye – Contributed to foveated rendering and eye-tracking integration, featured as one of TIME’s Best Inventions 2019.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon XR1 Platform – Supported hardware–software integration for XR prototypes.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 VR Development Kit – Assisted in building XR prototypes with advanced eye-tracking capabilities.
  • StarVR One – Contributed to development of a wide-FOV VR headset.
  • Tobii VR Development Kit – Built core eye-tracking platform for VR research and developer use.
  • Tobii IS4 Eye Tracking Platform – Advanced integration of eye-tracking in commercial applications.
  • Tobii IS3 Eye Tracking Platform – Early-stage development of eye-tracking systems for XR devices.

Impact: Multiple products contributed to were adopted globally, with two recognized in TIME’s Best Inventions.

Academic Research Projects

  • 3D Video Coding – KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden – Researched compression methods for immersive 3D video, improving streaming efficiency for XR applications.
  • Antarctic Sea Ice Edge Prediction – Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India – Applied machine learning techniques to climate data for accurate prediction of sea ice boundaries.
