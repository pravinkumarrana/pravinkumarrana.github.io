Projects.

Industry Projects

  • Pimax Crystal – High-end VR headset featuring integrated Tobii eye-tracking, enabling dynamic foveated rendering, auto-IPD adjustment, and enhanced social presence.
  • Tobii XR Eye Tracking Platform (Meta Butterscotch Varifocal) – Oversaw end-to-end developement of advanced eye-tracking platform that enables varifocal rendering for next-generation XR headsets.
  • Sony PlayStation VR2 – Integrated eye-tracking technology into a high-volume consumer gaming headset, enhancing immersion and user experience.
  • HP Reverb G2 Omnicept Edition VR – Enterprise VR headset with integrated Tobii eye-tracking and biometric sensors for immersive and data-driven XR experiences.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon XR2 5G Platform – Reference design platform enabling real-time eye-tracking for next-generation AR/VR devices.
  • Pico Neo 3 Pro Eye – Standalone VR headset with integrated Tobii eye-tracking, optimized for enterprise and immersive XR experiences.
  • Pico Neo 2 Eye – World’s first standalone VR headset with integrated Tobii eye-tracking, recognized as one of TIME’s Best Inventions 2020.
  • HTC Vive Pro Eye – World first VR headset with integrated eye-tracking from Tobii AB, featured as one of TIME’s Best Inventions 2019.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon XR1 Platform – XR1 reference design with integrated Tobii eye-tracking support for AR/VR prototypes.
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 VR Development Kit – Reference design for standalone XR prototypes with eye-tracking capabilities.
  • StarVR One – Integrated Tobii eye-tracking high-end enterprise VR headset with ultra-wide FOV.
  • Tobii VR Development Kit – Retro-fitted Tobii eye-tracking technology into the HTC Vive Business Edition to enable early developer adoption and build traction with OEMs.
  • Tobii IS4 Eye Tracking Platforma – World's first eye-tracking SoC (EyeChip) design for integration with Windows Hello–compliant, powering medical and consumer devices such as PRC-Saltillo Accent, Dell/Acer laptops, and Tobii EyeX.
  • Tobii IS3 Eye Tracking Platforma – Tobii’s first consumer-grade platform, deployed in devices such as Tobii EyeX, SteelSeries Sentry, and MSI GT72 gaming laptops.

Academic Research Projects

  • 3D Video Coding – KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden – Developed geometry-aware coding and rendering algorithms, improving view synthesis quality by up to 4 dB PSNR for immersive Free-viewpoint TV.
  • Antarctic Sea Ice Edge Prediction – Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India – Applied machine learning techniques to climate data for accurate prediction of sea ice boundaries.
