About me.

I am currently working as a Product Owner at Tobii AB, Sweden, a world leader in eye tracking technology and solutions. My role involves leading the development of eye tracking products and components in the XR segment and ensuring these products meet specified business value. Prior to this, 2014 - 2020, I worked as a Technical Lead/Scrum Master and Senior Algorithm Developer in the algorithm platform and integration team at Tobii AB. During this time, we successfully delivered the Tobii Eye Tracking VR Development Kit and integrated Tobii eye tracking technology into various platform such as HTC VIVE Pro Eye, StarVR One, Qualcomm Snapdragon Mobile and XR platforms, Pico Neo 2 Eye, and HP Reverb G2 Omnicept Edition VR. Since August 2017, I have been a certified Scrum Master by Scrum Alliance.

I am also pursuing a Telecommunications Doctorate at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden. Under the guidance of Markus Flierl, my research focuses on advancing 3D scene geometry, creating innovative view rendering algorithms, and designing novel data formats for the evolving field of free-viewpoint television. Ericsson AB and ACCESS Linnaeus Centre, KTH provided partial research support from 2008 to 2012.

I hold a Master of Technology in Earth System Science and Technology from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur, India and a Master of Science in Physics from Ranchi University, Ranchi, India. Additionally, I have experience as a Lecturer, teaching Physics at Government Polytechnic, Koderma, India for approximately one year.

Originally from Jhumri Telaiya, a small town located in northern Jharkhand, India. I have a keen interest in various areas such as image and video processing, free-viewpoint television, multiview video processing, camera-based eye tracking and gaze estimation, computer vision, and the application of machine learning in these domains.
